What is Bodybuilding Training?

Bodybuilding is a form of exercise focused on building muscle size, strength, and symmetry through resistance training and proper nutrition.

Benefits of Bodybuilding Training:

  • Increases muscle mass and strength.
  • Enhances physical appearance and body symmetry.
  • Improves bone density and joint health.
  • Boosts metabolism and aids in fat loss.
  • Promotes discipline through structured training and nutrition.
