What is Powerlifting Training?

Powerlifting is a strength sport focused on lifting maximum weight in three main exercises: squat, bench press, and deadlift.

Benefits of Powerlifting Training:

  • Builds maximal strength and power.
  • Improves bone density and overall joint health.
  • Enhances mental focus and resilience.
  • Develops full-body functional strength.
  • Supports long-term fitness with progressive overload.

Sample Powerlifting Training Split:

  • Monday: Bench
  • Tuesday: Deadlift
  • Wednesday: Bench
  • Thursday: Squat
  • Friday: Stretches + Foam Rolling
  • Saturday: Rest
  • Sunday: Repeat!
