What is CrossFit Training?

CrossFit is a strength and conditioning workout that is made up of functional movement performed at a high intensity level. These movements are actions that you perform in your day-to-day life, like squatting, pulling, pushing etc.

Benefits of CrossFit Training:

  • Improves overall fitness (strength, endurance, and flexibility)
  • Builds a strong sense of community and motivation
  • Time-efficient with short, high-intensity workouts.
  • Scalable for all fitness levels and abilities
  • Enhances functional movements for daily activities

Sample CrossFit Training Split:

  • Monday: Monday: Upper body + Conditioning
  • Tuesday: Tuesday: Lower body + Conditioning
  • Wednesday: Deadlift + Conditioning
  • Thursday: Thursday: Active rest & recovery
  • Friday: Upper Body + Aerobic Bodybuilding
  • Saturday: Lower body + Conditioning
  • Sunday: Rest
